Parking Allotment System

This app displays the availability of parking slots from anywhere.

It also updates the available parking slots automatically when a vehicle arrives on the parking slot.

Green color of parking slot signifies its availability and red color of parking slot signifies its unavailability.

It displays the available parking slots and the shortest among the available parking slots when the user is at gate.

It also stores the history of parkings associated with the user.
